Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a popular hand game played between two individuals. Players choose one of three gestures: rock, paper, or scissors. The objective is to defeat the opponent by selecting a gesture that overpowers theirs. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, and paper covers rock. It's a game of strategy, quick thinking, and anticipation. The winner is determined by the chosen gestures, and the game is often played for fun or as a simple decision-making tool.

TODO App

The TODO app is a handy tool for managing tasks and staying organized. It allows users to create, track, and prioritize their to-do items. With a simple interface, users can add new tasks, mark them as completed, and remove them when done. The app helps streamline productivity and ensures important tasks are not forgotten.
